YSC Diffusion Stock

AI-generated high-resolution imaging solution

YSC Diffusion Stock aims to share its extensive stock of AI-generated images with the general public, as well as the workflows used to generate them. We use diffusion models based on the base models of Stable Diffusion, an open-source generative AI created by Stability AI. The interface used to make such generations possible is ComfyUI, where the workflows made available will be for exclusive use for this interface.

YSC Diffusion Stock has a large catalog of AI-generated images, which can be viewed in the 'Images' tab. Here, the user is also able to send image requests directly to the generation server, which requires manual verification and generation, ensuring an accurate and hassle-free result.

Image catalogue

Extensive catalog with several high-resolution images, in addition to different types, artistic styles, among others. There is a greater focus on realistic images, but styles such as oil painting, drawings, vectors and anime can also be found. The images are displayed on the page as a low-resolution preview, with the image available for download in resolutions up to 4k.

Workflows

The images generated using Stable Diffusion models were generated using the ComfyUI interface, which operates based on interconnected nodes that execute the steps to generate an image. The interconnected and functional set of such nodes are called workflows. This page provides several workflows so that you can generate your own images according to your needs.

Requests

You can send us a form containing image requests. On this page you can detail what you want and an image will be generated according to your wishes. The time to receive the images may vary depending on the availability of our collaborators.
